# Brand Kit V1

## Purpose

This document records the selected brand kit direction for YSWORKS Web.

It defines the approved brand direction. Production assets and components remain
separate deliverables; official color values are governed by the repository
Design Tokens v1.0 contract.

## Brand Concept

Quiet Engineering.

Invisible complexity, visible simplicity.

YSWORKS should communicate that complex engineering work can result in clear, stable, and calm public experiences. The brand should feel precise and capable without exposing private systems or relying on spectacle.

## Logo Direction

The primary public signature is the `YSWORKS` wordmark. A compact mark, when a
surface requires one, uses the `YS` initials and never `Y + W`.

The mark should be:

- Simple.
- Geometric.
- Balanced.
- Recognizable at small sizes.
- Suitable for dark-first usage.
- Usable without explanatory text.

The monogram should feel engineered, not ornamental. It should avoid literal AI symbolism or technical clichés.

## Color Direction

The color direction is dark-first.

Use:

- A neutral base.
- High-contrast text.
- Restrained surfaces.
- The official YSWORKS Purple.

Primary 500, `#6D5EF7`, is the official brand color. It should be used
selectively for emphasis, focus, and identity moments. It should not dominate
the interface or become an exaggerated gradient system. The complete scale,
interaction colors, surfaces, and usage rules are defined in
[YSWORKS Design Tokens v1.0](../docs/design/YSWORKS_DESIGN_TOKENS_V1.md).

## Typography

Use Inter as the primary typeface.

Inter should carry:

- Body text.
- Navigation.
- Headings.
- Interface labels.

Use Geist Mono as the monospace typeface.

Geist Mono should be used for:

- Technical labels.
- Code-like references.
- Short system-style details.
- Small supporting moments where monospace improves clarity.

Monospace should remain an accent, not the primary voice of the brand.

## Visual Personality

YSWORKS should feel:

- Precise.
- Calm.
- Technical.
- Trustworthy.
- Premium.

Premium should come from restraint, spacing, typography, and consistency. It should not come from heavy effects or artificial polish.

## What To Avoid

Avoid:

- Neon or gamer style.
- Robots.
- Circuit boards.
- Fake AI imagery.
- Exaggerated gradients.
- Generic stock technology visuals.
- Artificial corporate scenes.
- Visuals that imply private systems are being shown.

The brand should not look like an AI novelty product or a generic technology template.

## Public and Private Boundary

YSWORKS is public.

YS AI OS remains private.

The brand may communicate engineering quality, public outcomes, and a calm technical point of view. It must not expose internal architecture, agents, prompts, workflows, infrastructure, business logic, or private implementation details.

## Usage Notes

The logo must work as:

- Favicon.
- GitHub avatar.
- Web mark.
- Banner element.
- Watermark.

The symbol should remain readable at small sizes.

The mark should be tested in:

- Light-on-dark usage.
- Dark-on-light usage.
- Single-color usage.
- Small square containers.
- Wide horizontal lockups.

Do not create final logo assets until the mark has been reviewed against these use cases.
