## Purpose

This directory is the future controlled home for approved YSWORKS brand assets.
It contains no production logo master at present.

Brand meaning and governance live in the
[Brand Bible](../docs/BRAND_BIBLE.md), subordinate to the
[Company Bible](../docs/COMPANY_BIBLE.md). Operational mapping lives in the
[Company Brand Book](../docs/company/brand-book.md). Construction and usage
requirements live in
[Visual Identity](../docs/company/visual-identity.md). Colour and interface
rules remain in the [Design Tokens](../docs/design/YSWORKS_DESIGN_TOKENS_V1.md)
and [Production Design System](../design-system/README.md).

## Structure

- [`logo/`](logo/README.md) — future approved vector masters and lock-ups.
- [`icons/`](icons/README.md) — future favicon and application-icon masters.
- [`templates/`](templates/README.md) — future approved document and
  presentation identity templates.
- [`exports/`](exports/README.md) — reproducible approved delivery exports.

## Asset Rules

- Do not add generated raster material as an official asset.
- Use **YS** as the constitutional monogram; treat `YW` only as an obsolete
  historical draft reference.
- Do not fabricate vector geometry from written direction alone.
- Preserve editable masters separately from generated exports.
- Record asset version, owner, approval, source, intended use, and checksum.
- Use predictable file names without client, secret, or environment data.
- Test contrast, clear space, minimum size, and light/dark use.
- Do not expose YS AI OS as an independent public brand.
- Do not place unapproved assets in production, Cloudflare, DNS, public pages,
  application manifests, or client systems.

## Approval Gate

An asset is production-approved only when the Founder has approved its geometry,
optical behaviour, variants, intended uses, and export set, and the applicable
documentation records that decision.
