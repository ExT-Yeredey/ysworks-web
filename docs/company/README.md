## Authority

The [Company Bible](../COMPANY_BIBLE.md) defines company identity, purpose,
philosophy, and enduring principles. The
[Master Specification](../YSWORKS_MASTER_SPEC.md) governs ecosystem architecture
and accepted cross-system decisions. The
[Founder Handbook](../FOUNDER_HANDBOOK.md) governs behaviour.

Documents in this folder apply those authorities to repeatable company work.
They do not override accepted ADRs, detailed security or portal contracts,
contracts, applicable law, or explicit Founder decisions.

## Document Rules

- Use British English and the official vocabulary.
- Link to higher authority instead of copying it.
- State purpose, scope, responsibility, exclusions, and required evidence.
- Distinguish policy, procedure, template, and engagement-specific terms.
- Record unknown or unapproved terms rather than inventing them.
- Keep client data, credentials, internal costs, private workflows, and
  production details outside this public repository.
- Review a document when its governing decision, legal context, or operating
  responsibility changes.

## Open Boundaries

The templates are operational foundations, not legal instruments. Final legal
terms, contracting entity, tax treatment, jurisdiction, payment controls,
refunds, data-processing terms, service levels, and signature requirements
require professional review and engagement-specific approval.
