# Brand Book

- Status: governed brand authority entry point
- Version: 1.0
- Owner: Founder

## Purpose

This document does not create a second brand system. It connects the
constitutional narrative to the approved brand authorities and defines how
brand decisions are governed.

Company character begins in the
[Company Bible](../COMPANY_BIBLE.md) (*I.I.1–5; I.VIII; I.XIV–XV*).
Identity, visual language, motion, interfaces, and continuity are governed by
the subordinate [Brand Bible](../BRAND_BIBLE.md)
(*II.I–VIII; II.X–XII; II.XV*). Detailed public positioning remains in
[Brand Positioning](../business/BRAND_POSITIONING.md). The canonical approved
operational source is [Brand Kit V1](../../.ai/brand-kit-v1.md), as mapped by the
[Approved Design Authorities](../design/README.md).

## Brand Foundation

- Company category: technology engineering company.
- Brand idea: Quiet Engineering.
- Official slogan: governed by the Company Bible (*I.I.5*).
- Character and perceptible qualities: governed by the Brand Bible
  (*II.II–III*).
- Communication and proof: governed by Volume I (*I.VIII*) and Volume II
  (*II.I; II.III; II.IX*).

## Identity System

The official constitutional monogram is **YS** (*II.VIII.1*). It is geometric,
minimal, scalable, and constructed rather than drawn. The operational identity
uses:

- the YS monogram with the YSWORKS wordmark as the primary logo;
- monogram-only use for compact approved applications; and
- one related visual family across YSWORKS products.

Earlier `YW` references were draft material and are obsolete. They are retained
only as historical evidence and must not govern a new asset or implementation.

Client Workspace uses the YSWORKS identity and has no separate independent
brand. “Client Portal” remains its technical architecture and security term.

YS AI OS is private internal infrastructure. It has no public logo, independent
public brand, or commercial identity, and its topology, prompts, agents,
telemetry, and internal records remain private (*I.VII.2*).

The generated raster reference is not a production asset. Final vectors,
lock-ups, clear-space rules, optical adjustments, favicon, app icon, social
assets, and export masters require a bounded Founder-reviewed construction task.
The [Branding Repository](../../branding/README.md) governs future assets.

## Colour And Typography

The constitutional role of YSWORKS Purple is guidance rather than decoration

in
[YSWORKS Design Tokens v1.0](../design/YSWORKS_DESIGN_TOKENS_V1.md).

Typography, spacing, layout, components, accessibility, and motion are governed
by the [Production Design System](../../design-system/README.md). This Brand Book
does not duplicate those values or the constitutional reasoning in Volume II.

## Voice

Use plain language, specific nouns, active voice, explicit limits, and
meaningful outcomes. Distinguish current, future, private, and unavailable
states.

Do not use unsupported superiority, autonomous-agent positioning, manufactured
urgency, generic AI imagery, fake proof, or claims that expose private systems.

## Media And Sound

- Photography follows the no-stock and authentic-subject doctrine
  (*II.IX.1–3*).
- Generated media may explain or argue, but it must never fabricate evidence or
  simulate factual reality (*II.XIV.2–4*).
- Every published generated artefact requires an accountable human reviewer.
- Sound remains silent by default; no operational audio asset exists until its
  necessity, accessibility, controls, ownership, and testing are approved
  (*II.XIII.1–6*).

## Governance

A brand change requires:

1. an identified need and affected surfaces;
2. consistency with the Company Bible and Brand Bible;
3. review against existing brand and design authorities;
4. accessible and production-appropriate assets;
5. Founder approval; and
6. versioned updates to every affected authority.

Implementation, a generated image, a design suggestion, or repeated informal use
does not establish a brand decision.
