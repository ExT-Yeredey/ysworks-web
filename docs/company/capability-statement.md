# Capability Statement

- Status: approved capability summary
- Owner: Founder
- Scope: current service capability, not implementation or guarantee

## Purpose

This statement explains the work YSWORKS can responsibly scope. The accepted
launch portfolio and exclusions remain governed by
[Master Specification section 3](../YSWORKS_MASTER_SPEC.md#3-services).

## Core Capability

YSWORKS translates business needs into secure, documented, and maintainable
digital systems. It combines analysis, architecture, implementation,
validation, delivery, and continuing support within an explicit responsibility
boundary.

## Service Areas

### AI Systems

Controlled AI-assisted capabilities tied to defined user or operational
outcomes, with evaluation, limitation, fallback, and human ownership.

Typical outputs may include use-case qualification, a product specification,
prototype, evaluation plan, data-boundary review, or approved AI-assisted
feature.

### Automation

Observable automation that reduces repetitive work and improves hand-offs
without obscuring ownership.

Typical outputs may include process mapping, automation design, integration,
validation rules, failure handling, operating documentation, and maintenance
scope.

### Software Development

Purpose-built software for an approved business or product need.

Typical outputs may include architecture, application components, integration
boundaries, tests, release evidence, documentation, and handover.

### Web Development

Accessible, performant, secure, and maintainable public or authenticated web
experiences.

Typical outputs may include information architecture, design-system
implementation, frontend engineering, content structures, validation, and
deployment documentation.

### Practical Cybersecurity

Defensive review, secure configuration, hardening, exposure reduction, access
control, and recovery planning within demonstrated capability.

YSWORKS does not claim accredited penetration testing, advanced offensive
security, or unrestricted security assurance.

### Infrastructure, Systems, And Professional Hardware

Supportable foundations for approved systems, including architecture,
configuration planning, networking, continuity, professional hardware, and
operational responsibility.

On-site delivery is primarily in Madrid and Lanzarote. Remote planning and
support may be available more broadly.

### Consulting

Independent technical analysis and decision support where a build is not yet
justified or another party will implement the result.

Typical outputs may include discovery findings, architecture options, risk
analysis, decision records, specifications, roadmaps, and review reports.

### Maintenance

Continuing responsibility through
[Essential Care, Growth Care, or Continuous Operations](maintenance-policy.md).
These are adaptable service levels, not rigid packages.

## Supporting Capability

Training and knowledge transfer may accompany an approved engagement. Training
is not an independent launch service category.

## Delivery Evidence

Capability is demonstrated through scope, qualified people, reviewable work,
validation, documentation, and honest limitations. YSWORKS does not use invented
metrics, client claims, testimonials, certifications, or internal tooling as
proof.

## Engagement Boundary

Every engagement specifies the outcome, assumptions, exclusions, client and
YSWORKS responsibilities, timing, evidence, security, support, maintenance, and
commercial terms. No capability statement creates a fixed price, availability
guarantee, or production authorisation.
