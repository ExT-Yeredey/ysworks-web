# Quotation Template

- Status: governed commercial-record template
- Owner: Founder or explicitly authorised commercial owner
- Classification: client-confidential when completed

## Use

Use this template to present engagement-specific charges that correspond to an
approved proposal scope. YSWORKS publishes no price list, range, starting price,
or standard rate through this repository.

This template is not accounting, tax, or legal advice. Final quotation terms
require review for the contracting entity, jurisdiction, currency, taxes, and
client.
It applies the [Company Bible](../COMPANY_BIBLE.md)
(*I.VIII.2; I.XV.2–3*) without creating public prices or commercial authority.

## 5. Assumptions And Exclusions

State:

- what the quotation includes;
- taxes and expenses treatment;
- third-party licences or services;
- client-provided materials or access;
- validity and schedule dependencies;
- changes requiring re-quotation; and
- excluded support, maintenance, travel, hardware, or procurement.

## 6. Maintenance Or Recurring Services

Identify the selected
[maintenance level](maintenance-policy.md), billing period, included
responsibility, review point, termination condition, and any separately approved
usage or third-party charge.

Do not describe adaptable maintenance levels as rigid packages.

## 7. Acceptance

Record the exact quotation reference and version, authorised client identity,
acceptance method, and timestamp. Acceptance is effective only with the related
proposal and required legal agreement.

No quotation authorises production access, infrastructure change, data
processing, or commencement before its stated conditions are met.
