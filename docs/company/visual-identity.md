# Visual Identity

- Status: governed identity usage specification
- Owner: Founder
- Scope: identity application, not final asset geometry

## Purpose

This document defines the safe use and future construction requirements of the
YSWORKS identity. It references, and does not duplicate, the
[Brand Book](brand-book.md), [Design Tokens](../design/YSWORKS_DESIGN_TOKENS_V1.md),
and [Production Design System](../../design-system/README.md).
Its constitutional basis is the
[Company Bible](../COMPANY_BIBLE.md) (*I.VII.2; I.VIII.2; I.XV.2*) and the
subordinate [Brand Bible](../BRAND_BIBLE.md) (*II.I; II.V; II.VIII; II.XV*).

## Official Direction

The primary identity combines the constitutional geometric **YS** monogram with
the YSWORKS wordmark. The monogram is angular, minimal, scalable, and
constructed rather than drawn.

Monogram-only use is reserved for favicon, application icon, compact product
marks, and the future Client Workspace where the context already identifies
YSWORKS.

Earlier `YW` references were obsolete draft material. They do not authorise a
logo, vector reconstruction, colour allocation, or production asset.

## Construction Requirement

Written direction cannot guarantee optical or geometric precision. Production
assets require a governed construction specification covering:

- source geometry and grid;
- angle, stroke, overlap, and negative-space relationships;
- optical correction at small sizes;
- minimum size and clear space;
- dark, light, and single-colour variants;
- primary and compact lock-ups;
- wordmark alignment;
- accessible contrast;
- favicon and application-icon simplification;
- SVG structure, naming, and metadata; and
- export formats and checksums.

No vector may be fabricated from prose alone. No generated raster may be traced
or shipped as the official mark without Founder-reviewed reconstruction and
testing.

## Usage Rules

- Preserve proportions, orientation, spacing, and approved colours.
- Use official assets from the [branding directory](../../branding/README.md).
- Do not stretch, rotate, outline, texture, shadow, or recolour the mark.
- Do not add AI symbols, circuits, robots, or unrelated product marks.
- Do not animate the logo decoratively.
- Do not place the mark where contrast or size compromises recognition.
- Keep YS AI OS within the same family without exposing it as a public brand.

## Validation

Before production approval, test the identity:

- at favicon and application-icon sizes;
- in primary horizontal and compact square contexts;
- on approved dark and light surfaces;
- in single-colour reproduction;
- in print and screen exports;
- at high zoom and standard display density; and
- with the full YSWORKS wordmark.

Final approval remains an open bounded brand task recorded in
[Business Open Decisions](../business/OPEN_DECISIONS.md).
