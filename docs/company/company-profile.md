# Company Profile

- Status: approved company summary
- Owner: Founder
- Audience: clients, suppliers, collaborators, and due-diligence reviewers

## Purpose

This profile provides a concise, public-safe description of YSWORKS. The
[Company Bible](../COMPANY_BIBLE.md) remains the constitutional narrative and
must be used when greater context is required.

## Company

YSWORKS is a technology engineering company. It designs, builds, secures,
documents, and maintains useful digital systems for organisations that need
clearer operations and dependable technical foundations.

The company works across software, web engineering, automation, controlled AI
systems, practical cybersecurity, infrastructure, professional hardware,
integration, consulting, and continuing maintenance.

## Working Principle

Every engagement begins with understanding the business need. Technology is
selected only after the intended outcome, users, constraints, risk, ownership,
and evidence are understood.

The company applies the
[central operating principle](../COMPANY_BIBLE.md#07-central-operating-principle)
to every engagement.

## Delivery Model

YSWORKS delivers bounded engagements with:

- explicit scope, exclusions, responsibilities, and acceptance evidence;
- reviewable engineering work;
- security, privacy, accessibility, and maintenance considered from the start;
- direct communication and visible limitations;
- documentation and handover as part of delivery; and
- an explicit decision to maintain, evolve, or close the engagement.

Physical and professional-hardware work is primarily available in Madrid and
Lanzarote. Suitable remote engineering, consulting, and support may be delivered
more broadly. Geography and availability are confirmed in each proposal.

## Public Boundaries

YSWORKS does not publish private client information, unsupported outcomes,
unapproved logos, invented metrics, internal costs, private workflows, or YS AI
OS internals.

Client work becomes public only with explicit permission, accurate evidence,
safe assets, and an approved description.

## Contact

The official public contact channel is `contact@ysworks.dev`. The Public Website
is bilingual in Spanish and English. Engagement terms are established through a
scoped proposal and agreement; public price lists are not used.
