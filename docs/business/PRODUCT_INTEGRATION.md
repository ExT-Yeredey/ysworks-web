# Product Integration

## Purpose

This document explains how future products and internal platforms may be
represented publicly without exposing private implementation.

## YS AI OS

YS AI OS remains private.

Public website treatment:

- describe it only as internal leverage when necessary;
- explain outcomes, not mechanisms;
- never expose architecture, agents, prompts, workflows, MCP servers,
  infrastructure, security model, or internal documentation;
- never imply clients can access it unless a separate public product is approved.

Recommended public framing:

> YSWORKS uses internal systems to improve quality, documentation, and delivery.

## Canarias Tourism Platform

Canarias Tourism Platform may become public later as:

- a Labs exploration;
- a product concept;
- a case study;
- a public product page.

It must not be presented as launched, commercially available, partnered, or
validated until those facts are true and approved.

Public content should avoid:

- private product architecture;
- unapproved partners;
- invented metrics;
- proprietary route or personalization logic;
- confidential roadmap.

## Products Owned By YSWORKS

A product may receive a public page when:

- it exists;
- public scope is approved;
- pricing or access status is clear;
- privacy and security posture are reviewed;
- support expectations are documented.

## Services And Products

Keep service pages and product pages separate.

Services describe client work YSWORKS can perform.

Products describe public software or resources YSWORKS owns and offers.

## Integration Order

1. Services.
2. Labs.
3. Journal.
4. Real work.
5. Public products.

Do not invert the order by promoting products before they are ready.
