# SEO Strategy

## Purpose

SEO should help the right people understand YSWORKS. It should not encourage
clickbait, fake authority, or low-quality AI content.

## Technical SEO

Required foundations:

- canonical production URL;
- page-specific titles and descriptions;
- `robots.txt`;
- `sitemap.xml`;
- Open Graph metadata;
- social image when brand assets exist;
- clean heading hierarchy;
- semantic HTML;
- internal links without dead routes;
- fast static pages.

## Keyword Themes

Use careful, realistic themes:

- AI engineering studio.
- AI integration.
- intelligent automation.
- web platform engineering.
- secure web development.
- product engineering.
- technical consulting.
- automation consulting.
- Cloudflare Pages website.
- Astro website development.

Keywords should be refined after real positioning, services, and geography are
approved.

## Landing Pages

Create landing pages only when each has distinct value.

Avoid many thin pages. Prefer fewer pages with strong content.

## Schema

Future schema may include:

- `Organization`;
- `WebSite`;
- `Service`;
- `Article`;
- `BreadcrumbList`.

Do not add schema that claims reviews, ratings, clients, products, or offers that
do not exist.

## Robots And Sitemap

The site should include:

- `public/robots.txt`;
- generated or static sitemap;
- no indexing of private or preview-only routes.

## Long-Term Strategy

SEO should grow through useful public writing, real services, and approved proof.

Do not publish AI-generated filler content.
