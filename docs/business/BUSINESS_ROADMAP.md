# Business Roadmap

## Purpose

This roadmap defines commercial evolution for YSWORKS. It is not a private
technical roadmap.

Growth remains constrained by
[YSWORKS Enterprise Architecture](../YSWORKS_ENTERPRISE_ARCHITECTURE.md)
(*EA 11.1–3*): systems, documentation, architecture, automation, delegation,
then people. The phases below do not claim that any enterprise system or
control is implemented.

## Phase 1: Presence

Goal: make YSWORKS understandable and credible.

Work:

- homepage refinement;
- core public pages;
- canonical URL and SEO basics;
- privacy page;
- clear contact path;
- no broken navigation;
- trust principles.

## Phase 2: Content

Goal: build public credibility without fake proof.

Work:

- services pages;
- Journal;
- Labs;
- public writing cadence;
- work principles page;
- approved case studies only when real.

## Phase 3: Lead Generation

Goal: support qualified inbound interest.

Work:

- contact form;
- Turnstile;
- honeypot;
- rate limiting;
- privacy-aware analytics;
- newsletter only if useful;
- downloadable public resources if appropriate.

## Phase 4: Products

Goal: introduce public products only when ready.

Work:

- product pages;
- public documentation;
- support expectations;
- pricing or access status;
- safe demos;
- clear separation from private systems.

## Phase 5: Scale

Goal: grow sustainably while preserving quality.

Work:

- more real case studies;
- open-source projects when useful;
- stronger content system;
- improved observability;
- mature brand assets;
- refined sales process.

## Roadmap Rules

- Do not publish private roadmap details.
- Do not present future products as launched.
- Do not create pages without content.
- Keep quality higher than output volume.
